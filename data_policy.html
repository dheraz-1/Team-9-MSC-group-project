Data policy

  • Data Collection: We will collect data from you voluntarily submitted during the course of the project, such as age, gender (limited to male or female), and other relevant information. We may also collect data from publicly available sources or third-party data providers, strictly adhering to applicable laws and regulations.
  • Data Usage: The data collected will be used solely for the purpose of the age and gender prediction project, specifically limited to predicting age and gender as either male or female based on the provided data. It will be used to develop and train machine learning algorithms for this specific classification purpose. The data will be anonymized and aggregated, and individual identities will be protected.
  • Data Security: We will take reasonable measures to protect the collected data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. By participating in this project, you acknowledge and accept the inherent risks of data transmission over the internet.
  • Data Sharing: We may share the aggregated and anonymized data with third parties, such as academic researchers or collaborators, for the purpose of scientific research, as long as it does not disclose any personally identifiable information.
  • Consent: Your participation in this project is entirely voluntary. By providing your data, you are giving your explicit consent for us to collect, use, and share your data as described in this data policy disclaimer.
  • Rights to Withdraw: You have the right to withdraw your participation in this project at any time without providing any reason. You also have the right to request deletion of your data from our records by contacting us.
  • Legal Compliance: We will comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding data privacy and protection, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and will take necessary steps to ensure data privacy and security.
  • Updates: This data policy disclaimer may be updated from time to time, and any changes will be communicated to you through the project website or other means of communication.

By participating in this age and gender prediction project, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this data policy disclaimer, and agree to comply with its terms and conditions. If you have any questions or concerns about this data policy, please contact us for further clarification.
